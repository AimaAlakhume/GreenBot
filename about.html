About

GreenBot educates New York City residents
on how environmentally friendly or 'green' their
borough is. Doing so sheds light on the
average New Yorker's impact on our city.
Then, GreenBot shares resources to help them
get more involved.

The Department of Sanitation New York
(DSNY) has a significant number of recycling and
donating initiatives. However, people often don't
know about these programs. GreenBot aims to
change that.
